ArmIT is the IT section's business relations committee, we work to build a clear connection between the IT students and potential future employers. Among other things ArmIT organizes Lunch seminars and mingle nights with companies that are interesting for IT students. As a company wanting to get in contact with our students, you become assigned to a contact in ArmIT in order to give you tailored marketing towards the students and give the students the best possible image of you and your business.

ArmIT is also working with programs Computer science and Electrical engineering when organizing the career fair, DatE-IT, which takes place each fall in November.
